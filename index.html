How It Works

Our Booking Process

  1. Consultation: Share your concept, reference images, and placement.
  2. Design: We prepare a draft aligned with your skin tone and body flow.
  3. Session: Sterile setup, precise stencil prep, and focused execution.

Studio Standards

Aftercare & Policies

Aftercare Basics

Clean gently 2-3x daily, use light aftercare balm, avoid soaking and direct sun during healing.

Booking Policy

Appointment slot is secured by deposit. Design revisions are finalized before session day.

Session Prep

Sleep well, hydrate, and eat before your schedule. Avoid alcohol before and immediately after your session.
